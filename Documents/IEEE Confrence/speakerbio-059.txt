Mr. Ishan Pathak received his B.Tech degree in Computer Science and Engineering from G.D. Goenka University, Gurgaon, India, in 2021. He is currently pursuing his M.S. degree in Computer Science department at Texas A&M University–Kingsville, where he also serves as a Research Assistant at the Cyber-Physical Power and Energy Systems Laboratory (CPPES).
